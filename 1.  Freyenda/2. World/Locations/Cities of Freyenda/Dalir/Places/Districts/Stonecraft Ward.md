# **Stonecraft Ward**

Stonecraft Ward holds Dalir's master forges, carving courts, and guildhalls. Here, Iron Peaks stone becomes fortresswork, statuary, and the carved cores of Dalir's ships. The [[Stonewrights Circle]] keeps strict standards and detailed records.
