# **Lowtide Warrens**

Lowtide Warrens is a cramped district of workshops, boarding houses, and hidden alleys near the eastern sea walls. It is a haven for smugglers and the base of the [[Graywake Circle]]. Residents whisper that the fog here carries secrets.
